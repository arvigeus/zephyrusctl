# Code of Conduct

## Expected behavior

Engage in good faith. Critique ideas, not people. When you disagree, say why — clearly and without hostility or condescension.

That covers most of it. When in doubt, ask yourself if you'd say it to someone you look up to — if not, don't write it.

## Participating in the discussion

Contributions speak louder than plain opinions; what counts is code, funding, or sustained use. Opinions alone carry no obligation to act on them. If your vision differs, fork it and take ownership.
